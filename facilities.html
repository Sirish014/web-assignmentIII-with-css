Training Ground Gym Rehabilitation
  1. Training Pitches: The complex boasts 9 football pitches, including 5 natural grass pitches and 4 artificial grass pitches. These pitches cater to various training needs, from 11-a-side matches to smaller 7-a-side games2.
  2. Multisport Arena: There's a multisport pavilion that accommodates basketball, futsal, and handball.
  3. Training Areas: Dedicated areas for goalkeepers and technical work, ensuring specialized training for all players.
  1. Modern Equipment: The gym features a wide range of equipment, including weight machines, free weights, cardiovascular machines (treadmills, bikes, rowing machines), and functional training tools like kettlebells, resistance bands, and medicine balls.
  2. Training Zones: It is divided into various zones for different types of workouts, including strength training, cardiovascular conditioning, and functional training.
  3. Technological Integration: The gym incorporates the latest sports science technologies, including motion analysis systems and performance monitoring tools, allowing for precise tracking of each player's progress.
  4. Personalized Training: Players receive personalized workout plans tailored to their specific needs, whether they are focusing on injury prevention, strength building, or cardiovascular fitness.
  5. Support Staff: A team of fitness coaches and trainers is always on hand to assist players with their workouts, ensuring they use the equipment correctly and achieve their fitness goals.
  1. Physiotherapy Rooms: Equipped with the latest physiotherapy equipment, these rooms are used for manual therapy, ultrasound therapy, electrotherapy, and other treatments.
  2. Hydrotherapy Pools: Hydrotherapy plays a vital role in recovery, offering resistance and buoyancy to help reduce pain and improve mobility during the healing process.
  3. Recovery Areas: These areas are equipped with saunas, steam rooms, and cryotherapy chambers to assist with muscle recovery and reduce inflammation.
  4. Medical Staff: A team of sports doctors, physiotherapists, and rehabilitation specialists provides expert care and develops individualized treatment plans for each player.
  5. Diagnostics: The center is equipped with advanced diagnostic tools, including MRI and ultrasound machines, to accurately assess injuries and monitor recovery progress.
