Training and Development

La Masia offers comprehensive football training and development programs for young talents, focusing on technical skills, tactical understanding, and physical conditioning.

Education and Academics

Our academy places a strong emphasis on education, ensuring that all players receive formal schooling alongside their football training. This holistic approach prepares them for life both on and off the pitch.

Personal Development

We nurture the personal growth of our players by instilling values such as teamwork, discipline, and respect. Mentoring and psychological support are provided to help them navigate the challenges of professional sports.

Charity Support

Our orgainization always tries to give something to the community by organizing some charity matches. By giving the ticket incomes generated from games to the needy destroyed by the climaties. Previously, Barcelona has colaborated with UNICEF to help the children in the education and health sector.
